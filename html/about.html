About us

At SoccerPark, product quality and customer satisfaction are the most important virtues to us. Each SoccerPark location is carefully vetted, analyzed and monitored to ensure optimum player experience. From the parking facility to the service provided by the staff, we ensure our customers leave, only to come back. We use European artificial turf, handcrafted by the finest European Turf manufactures in the world and laid out by the most experienced turf installers in the country.

All our turf comes from FIFA Recommended vendors only. Customer safety is of utmost importance, therefore we take utmost care and make sure all our locations are safe and sound. Our staff is sure to make you feel comfortable and take care of everything else while you focus on your game. Since everyone deserves a break, feel free to enjoy our beverages on a hot summer day and under a heavily flood lit arena. Last but not the least, our locations are easy to access and in the center of the city. Enjoy a game at SoccerPark and feel free to let us know how it was at feedback@soccerpark.com
